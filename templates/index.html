Dune: Imperium

The board game of political intrigue and galactic conquest.

Features

  • Control one of six great factions in the Dune universe.
  • Manage your resources and build your army.
  • Negotiate with other players to form alliances and betrayals.
  • Wage war on your opponents to conquer the galaxy.

Gameplay

Dune: Imperium is a competitive strategy game for 2-6 players. Each player takes control of one of six great factions in the Dune universe: the Atreides, Harkonnen, Corrino, Bene Gesserit, Fremen, or Spacing Guild. Players must manage their resources, build their army, and negotiate with other players to form alliances and betrayals. The ultimate goal of the game is to conquer the galaxy by controlling the majority of the game's six victory points.
